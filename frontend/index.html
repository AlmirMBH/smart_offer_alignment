Bill of Quantities Analyzer

Compare bills of quantities in one file

Upload several Excel files, then export one CSV with the same work from each offer on one row.

How it works

  1. 1 Upload Upload Excel troškovnici for a trade (e.g. piping). The app detects relevant sheets, parses each line, and saves it. Nothing is merged at upload.
  2. 2 Price Prices from Excel are kept. Missing prices can be suggested from the web. You review and approve each line on the Price Approvals tab.
  3. 3 Export When you download the comparison CSV, the app merges similar lines from different files into one row (similarity threshold in Settings). Piping and electro never mix.
  4. 4 CSV One row per matched item, columns per offer (unit, quantity, prices). The merged column is True when two or more offers share that row. Combined descriptions are joined with ||. Only approved prices are exported.

Upload bill of quantities

Select a category and upload your Excel troškovnici. The app detects relevant sheets, saves each line, and suggests prices where Excel has none. Nothing is merged at upload. Use Export above to merge similar lines from all uploaded files into one CSV. Adjust how strict matching is under Settings. Only approved prices are written to the export.
